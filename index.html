I’m an economist working across research and policy. Currently, I work for the German Expert Commission for Research and Innovation, where I contribute to applied policy work on how to strengthen innovation systems.

I'm interested in market design, innovation policy, and the governance of research systems to align innovation incentives with social welfare.

My background includes a PhD and postdoctoral research in microeconomics and experimental methods, as well as some experience in consulting.

I like deep, solution-oriented thinking, and I’m always up for a coffee (in Berlin or virtually) to talk about good policy, new ideas, or what you’re working on!
